About

Over two decades of experience, loans done right the first time.

Buying your first home doesn’t have to feel overwhelming and you don’t have to figure it out alone. I specialize in helping first-time homebuyers understand their options, from low down payment programs to down payment assistance that can make homeownership more accessible than you think.

I take a step-by-step approach, walking you through the entire process from prequalification to closing so you feel informed, confident, and in control every step of the way. Whether you’re just starting to explore or ready to make a move, I’m here to guide you.
